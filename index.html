How to play

1Shake — the shaker gives the phone a really good shake.
2Splash! — the pool is revealed for just a few seconds. Memorize the dice!
3Pick — pass the phone around; everyone secretly picks ONE category they haven’t scored yet.
4Score — the pool is uncovered and points are added automatically.

12 rounds, and each category can be scored only once — in round 12 your last category scores automatically. Highest total wins; ties are broken by the Pool Party score.
